Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Cocktail attire.

Ladies, although the ceremony will be on a patio and the reception indoors, we recommend you wear comfortable shoes so you can last the whole night on the dance floor. We will also have lawn games outside!

Gentlemen, ties are completely optional.

Are children allowed?

Yes. We can’t wait to see them on the dance floor!

Is there parking on the premises? How much is it?

Yes. There is plenty of free parking on-site, but it’s on non-paved gravel.

Is the venue ADA accessible?

Yes. The ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception areas are all on the same level and wheelchair accessible. If you have any special needs, please don't hesitate to reach out in advance. We want to make sure everyone has a great time.
