Welcome to D.C.

We are so grateful and excited to have you with us on our special day! Below is everything you need to know to make the most of your time in the D.C. area.

Where to Land

Closest airport to downtown Washington, D.C.

Drive time to venue:
~60 minutes
Dulles International Airport (IAD)

Where to Stay

BOOK A ROOM

Best Western Leesburg Hotel and Conference Center

A block of rooms has been reserved for our guests at a discounted rate. Reference the Rodriguez/O'Donnell Wedding when making your reservation. This hotel is about a 20-minute drive from the wedding venue.

BROWSE RENTALS

Live Like the Locals. Airbnb in Purcellville, VA

There are plenty of beautiful properties available for rent on Airbnb, including a tiny house if you're feeling adventurous! The wedding venue is in Purcellville so all of these properties should be fairly close.
